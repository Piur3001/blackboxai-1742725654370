About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to art education

Our History

Manas Art Academy was founded by the Senate in 2007. Our journey began with the Departments of Painting and Graphic Design in 2009, welcoming our first cohort of talented students.

In 2015, we expanded our offerings by opening the Departments of Music and Theater, further enriching our academic community and providing more opportunities for artistic expression and growth.

Our Mission

Education Excellence

Providing high-quality education that meets international standards and prepares students for successful careers in the arts.

Artistic Innovation

Fostering creativity and innovation while preserving and promoting cultural heritage through artistic expression.

Global Perspective

Creating opportunities for international collaboration and exchange in the arts community.

Academic Programs

Our academic programs cover 8 semesters and have been approved by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Kyrgyz Republic. The program is similar to equivalent programs in Kyrgyzstan and Turkey, meeting Bologna Process requirements.

Program Features

  • 8 semester comprehensive curriculum
  • International exchange opportunities
  • Professional development workshops
  • Exhibition and performance opportunities

Student Benefits

  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities
  • Mentorship from experienced faculty
  • Industry connections and networking
  • Career guidance and support
