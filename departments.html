Our Departments

Explore our diverse range of artistic disciplines

Department of Painting

The Department of Painting offers comprehensive training in various painting techniques and styles. Students develop their artistic vision while mastering traditional and contemporary approaches to painting.

Course Highlights

Oil painting, watercolor, contemporary techniques, art history, and composition

Career Opportunities

Professional artist, art educator, gallery curator, art restorer

Department of Graphic Design

The Department of Graphic Design combines artistic creativity with technical skills to prepare students for careers in digital design and visual communication.

Course Highlights

Digital design, typography, branding, web design, and motion graphics

Career Opportunities

Graphic designer, UI/UX designer, art director, brand designer

Department of Music

The Department of Music offers comprehensive training in music theory, performance, and composition. Students develop their musical abilities while exploring various genres and traditions.

Course Highlights

Music theory, composition, performance, music history, and ensemble playing

Career Opportunities

Professional musician, music educator, composer, music producer

Department of Theater

The Department of Theater provides comprehensive training in acting, directing, and theatrical production. Students develop their performance skills while exploring various aspects of theater arts.

Course Highlights

Acting techniques, directing, stage design, theater history, and production

Career Opportunities

Actor, director, theater educator, production designer
